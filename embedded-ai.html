Embedded AI & IoT Engineer.
Job description

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Embedded AI & IoT Engineer to design, develop, and deploy intelligent embedded systems. In this role, you will be instrumental in taking AI models from concept to production on resource-constrained devices, enabling real-time data processing and decision-making at the edge. The ideal candidate is passionate about bridging the gap between embedded hardware and intelligent software to create robust and scalable IoT products.

Responsibilities
  • Embedded AI Development: Design, implement, and optimize machine learning models for deployment on edge devices using platforms like EdgeImpulse, TensorFlow Lite for Microcontrollers, or similar frameworks.
  • Full-Stack Embedded Development: Architect and develop firmware for microcontroller-based systems (e.g., ESP32, Raspberry Pi), including driver development, sensor integration, power management, and peripheral communication protocols (I2C, SPI, UART).
  • IoT System Architecture: Design and implement secure and efficient data pipelines for IoT devices, including connectivity to cloud platforms (e.g., AWS IoT, Google Cloud IoT Core, Azure IoT Hub, MQTT brokers) and on-premise systems like Home Assistant.
  • Data Management & Visualization: Develop systems for collecting, storing, and analyzing sensor data. Create and maintain dashboards for real-time device monitoring, remote management, and data-driven insights.
  • System Integration & Automation: Develop and configure automation rules and integrations within IoT ecosystems to enable seamless device interaction and intelligent behaviors.
  • Lifecycle Engineering: Own the entire development lifecycle—from prototyping and testing to deployment, maintenance, and troubleshooting—ensuring high reliability, performance, and security standards.
  • Collaboration & Documentation: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including cloud, software, and hardware engineers. Produce clear, concise, and comprehensive design documentation and technical reports.
Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • 2+ years of professional experience in embedded systems development with a focus on IoT and AI applications.
  • Proficiency in embedded C/C++ and Python programming languages.
  • Hands-on experience with microcontroller platforms (e.g., ESP32/8266, ARM Cortex-M, STM32, Raspberry Pi) and real-time operating systems (RTOS).
  • Demonstrable experience with embedding ML models (TinyML) on resource-constrained devices.
  • Solid understanding of IoT communication protocols (e.g., MQTT, HTTP, WebSockets, CoAP) and wireless technologies (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth/BLE).
  • Experience with version control (Git), CI/CD pipelines, and embedded debugging tools (JTAG, logic analyzers).
  • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and debugging skills.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
Qualifications
  • Experience configuring and customizing IoT hubs and home automation platforms (e.g., Home Assistant, OpenHAB).
  • Familiarity with IoT cloud services (e.g., AWS IoT, Google Cloud IoT, Azure IoT Suite, Firebase).
  • Knowledge of containerization (Docker) and virtualization technologies for development and testing.
  • Experience with data visualization tools (Grafana, Node-RED) and time-series databases.
  • A portfolio of personal or professional projects (GitHub, blog, etc.) demonstrating relevant skills.
