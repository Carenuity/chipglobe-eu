AI Team Lead
Job description

We are seeking a dynamic and experienced AI Team Lead to spearhead the design and deployment of AI-powered solutions across embedded systems, IoT devices, and cloud platforms at Chipglobe. This role combines deep technical expertise in electronics and embedded system development with leadership in AI/ML strategy, human resource development, and cross-functional team management. As AI Team Lead, you will mentor engineers, drive innovation, and ensure the delivery of scalable, secure, and impactful AI-driven products. This position is ideal for a leader who thrives at the intersection of AI innovation, embedded hardware/software integration, and people development.

Responsibilities
  • Lead, mentor, and grow a multidisciplinary AI team, including embedded engineers, data scientists, and software developers.
  • Architect, develop, and deploy AI-driven solutions for IoT devices, edge systems, and real-time embedded platforms.
  • Oversee electronics and embedded system development, ensuring seamless hardware-software integration.
  • Define AI/ML strategies, including data acquisition, model training, optimization, and edge deployment.
  • Align AI initiatives with organizational goals and client needs, balancing technical excellence with business value.
  • Drive integration of AI into embedded hardware, cloud ecosystems, and smart device applications.
  • Manage human resource development within the AI team: recruitment, skills training, performance reviews, and career growth planning.
  • Conduct technical reviews, ensure compliance with security and reliability standards, and foster a culture of continuous learning.
  • Keep the team updated with emerging AI frameworks, embedded technologies, and industry best practices.
Requirements
  • Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Systems, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
  • Proven expertise in AI/ML development and deployment, with hands-on experience in Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch, and edge AI frameworks.
  • Strong background in electronics, embedded systems (ESP32, STM32, Raspberry Pi), and hardware integration.
  • Demonstrated experience in team leadership, human resource management, and staff development.
  • Knowledge of IoT systems, data pipelines, APIs, and microservices.
  • Solid understanding of software and hardware security, system optimization, and real-time applications.
  • Excellent problem-solving, communication, and organizational leadership skills.
Qualifications
  • Contributions to AI, IoT, or embedded systems projects (open-source, research, or industrial).
  • Experience with DevOps, CI/CD, containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Familiarity with real-time communication protocols (MQTT, BLE, Zigbee, LoRa).
  • Background in computer vision, sensor fusion, or AI for smart devices.
What We Offer
  • Opportunity to lead high-impact AI + embedded systems projects shaping the future of smart devices.
  • A collaborative and innovation-driven environment with global exposure.
  • Professional growth through leadership development, continuous learning, and cutting-edge research.
  • Competitive compensation and benefits package.
  • Flexible work arrangements (remote/hybrid depending on location).
