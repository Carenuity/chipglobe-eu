PCB Design and Development Engineer.
Job description

We are looking for a highly skilled and detail-oriented PCB Design and Development Engineer to join our innovative team.You will play a critical role in transforming electronic circuit concepts into high-quality, production-ready printed circuit boards. The ideal candidate is passionate about electronics, excels in a collaborative environment, and is driven to optimize designs for performance, reliability, and manufacturability.

Responsibilities
  • Design and develop sophisticated PCB layouts from concept to production using CAD tools like KiCad and Altium Designer.
  • Create and manage schematics, component footprints, and board layouts, ensuring optimal component placement and routing.
  • Collaborate with the engineering team to unify various development boards (e.g., ESP32 variants) and microcontrollers into custom, integrated PCB solutions.
  • Optimize designs for signal integrity, power distribution, thermal management, and EMI/EMC compliance.
  • Generate and meticulously verify all fabrication and assembly files (Gerber, BOM, pick-and-place).
  • Conduct thorough design reviews and implement feedback and revisions to meet precise project specifications.
  • Test prototypes, troubleshoot hardware issues, and refine designs for enhanced performance.
  • Maintain rigorous version control and comprehensive documentation for all projects.
Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical/Electronics Engineering or a related field.
  • Proven proficiency in PCB design software (e.g., KiCad, Altium Designer, or similar).
  • Strong understanding of electronics principles, embedded systems, and microcontroller integration.
  • Solid experience with high-speed signal routing, grounding techniques, and component library management.
  • Ability to interpret complex datasheets, schematics, and technical drawings.
  • Familiarity with Design for Manufacturability (DFM) and Design for Test (DFT) principles.
  • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and troubleshooting skills.
  • Keen attention to detail with the ability to deliver high-quality work under tight deadlines.
Qualifications
  • Hands-on experience designing PCBs for IoT devices.
  • Background in designing for renewable energy systems, power electronics, or automation.
  • Proven experience in prototype testing, debugging, and validation.

If you are interested in this position, please send your CV and the reason why you would like to join Chipglobe to: employment@chipglobe.com
