Case Designer (3D Printing & Marketing Visualization)
Job description

We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Case Designer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for designing innovative product cases and enclosures, producing functional and aesthetic prototypes through 3D printing, and developing high-quality visualization assets for marketing. This role combines industrial design expertise, additive manufacturing skills, and visual communication to deliver both product and promotional value.

Responsibilities
  • Design and develop product cases, enclosures, and components for various applications.
  • Create precise 3D models and technical drawings optimized for additive manufacturing.
  • Operate and maintain 3D printers to produce prototypes and small-scale production runs.
  • Develop high-quality visualizations, renderings, and animations for marketing and product presentations.
  • Collaborate with engineers, product managers, and marketing teams to ensure design feasibility and brand consistency.
  • Research emerging trends in design, materials, and 3D printing technologies.
  • Ensure functionality, durability, and manufacturability of all designs.
Requirements
  • Proven experience as a Case Designer, Industrial Designer, or Product Designer (minimum 1 year)
  • Proficiency with 3D CAD software (SolidWorks, Fusion 360, Blender, or Rhino).
  • Hands-on experience with 3D printing technologies (FDM, SLA, or SLS).
  • Strong visualization and rendering skills (KeyShot, Blender, or equivalent).
  • Understanding of materials, finishing techniques, and design for manufacturing.
  • Ability to create marketing-ready visual assets (renders, infographics, animations).
  • Strong communication, collaboration, and problem-solving skills.
Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Industrial Design, Product Design, Mechanical Engineering, or related field (or equivalent practical experience).
  • 1+ year of professional experience in product/case design and 3D printing.
  • Portfolio showcasing both 3D design/printing projects and marketing visualization work.
  • Bonus:
    • Experience with graphic design tools (Adobe Creative Suite, Figma).
    • Knowledge of electronics enclosure standards.
    • Familiarity with marketing content creation and brand storytelling.
