Verification Services

Verification Services

Depending on the client needs, we are offering a wide range of services in order to conduct the verification process in a given setup, while respecting the project milestones. Having worked with a variety of clients, from startups to large semiconductor companies, we are able to provide consulting and support in the planning stages of a project as well as to jump in and help the ongoing verification process in the optimal way.

Languages, tools and more

Having experts with 20+ years of experience across the teams, we provide expertise in many different existing setups and are able to build one from scratch. While excelling both in UVM and eRM, in cases where an approach based on an existing verification methodology is not applicable we define and document guidelines on our own.

  • Specman e
  • SystemVerilog
  • SVA, PSL
  • C, C++, SystemC
  • Assembly languages
  • Perl, TCL, Ruby, Makefile and Python scripting

Verification categories

  • Functional verification
  • Formal verification
  • FW verification
  • Mixed-signal verification
  • Functional safety
  • Portable Stimulus

Tools

  • Cadence
  • Synopsys
  • Mentor
  • JIRA and other bug/issue tracking tools

Different levels and approaches

Our verification experts are always ready for the next challenge, whether it is a verification process starting from the device concept phase, defining and executing a methodology transition or performing upgrades, maintenance and support. Starting from module level and all the way through to the SoC level of verification, we are offering structured and documented project execution providing limitless possibilities of re-use and upgrades in the next product cycles.

  • Verification environment development/transition/upgrade
  • Test development, transition, and/or upgrade
  • 3rd party VIP usage and/or VIP development
  • Metric driven functional verification
  • Coverage closure
