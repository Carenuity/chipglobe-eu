ChipGlobe Verification Services

Welcome to ChipGlobe's Functional Verification Services page, where reliability, precision, and efficiency are at the core of everything we do. At ChipGlobe, we understand the importance of verifying designs at every level, from initial concepts to final production, ensuring that every chip meets the highest standards of quality and functionality.

Our Expertise in Chip Design

ChipGlobe specializes in a comprehensive range of verification services, including Functional Verification, Mixed Signal Verification, and SoC/IP Verification. Our experienced team utilizes state-of-the-art methodologies such as UVM (Universal Verification Methodology), Constraint Random Coverage-Driven Verification, and Power Aware Verification to ensure that all functional aspects of your chip design meet the desired specifications.

Whether you need support for building a testbench architecture, or you need top-level verification using proven methodologies, our team can deliver flexible solutions customized for your needs. With deep expertise in languages like SystemVerilog, Specman e, VHDL, and tools from Cadence, Mentor, and Synopsys, we ensure robust verification to meet even the most demanding requirements.

Our Verification Categories

  • Module to SoC-Level Verification: Starting from individual modules to full-chip (SoC) verification, we handle the entire verification lifecycle.
  • Test Development and Methodology Transition: We assist with setting up, transitioning, and upgrading verification methodologies, leveraging our extensive experience with Cadence Questa, Synopsys VCS, and other industry-leading environments.
  • Verification Environment Development: From scratch or as an upgrade to an existing environment, our structured and documented approach ensures seamless integration.
  • Power-Aware Verification: We incorporate power verification into your flow, including UPF (Unified Power Format) and low-power simulations to ensure that your designs are both energy-efficient and functionally reliable.
  • Coverage Closure and Metric-Driven Verification: We utilize advanced techniques to ensure comprehensive coverage closure, leaving no stone unturned.

Tools and Technologies We Use

We work with various industry-leading tools and methodologies to ensure that our verification services are of the highest quality:

  • Verification Languages: SystemVerilog, Specman e, VHDL, Verilog, Verilog-AMS, System C, C++, TLM.
  • Verification Methodologies: UVM/OVM, Constraint Random Verification, Metric-Driven Verification, Functional Safety according to ISO 26262, Portable Stimulus.
  • EDA Vendor Tools: Cadence, Synopsys, Mentor, as well as JIRA for bug tracking and project management.

ChipGlobe's Global Presence

With design centers across the globe, including in Munich (Germany), Dresden (Germany), Belgrade (Serbia), Thessaloniki (Greece), Sofia (Bulgaria), Singapore, and Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam), ChipGlobe is positioned to provide support wherever you are. Our distributed teams collaborate closely with customers, offering local expertise and ensuring that the unique requirements of each region are met.

Our Verification Approach

Our verification approach is designed to address the unique challenges faced by each of our clients:

  • End-to-End Support: From module-level to SoC-level verification, we provide a structured project execution process, ensuring high quality and opportunities for reuse in future product development cycles.
  • Integration with Client Teams: Our experts often work on-site or through well-integrated ODC (Offshore Development Center) models, providing seamless integration with your existing teams.
  • Flexible and Scalable Solutions: We understand that every project has its specific requirements, and we adapt our solutions to provide flexibility while maintaining a focus on scalability for future upgrades and iterations.
  • Quality Assurance: Certified with ISO 9001:2015 for Quality Management Systems, we emphasize quality across all aspects of verification.
