Front-End Developer & UI/UX Designer
Job description

We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Front-End Developer & UI/UX Designer to join our team. You will be responsible for designing intuitive user experiences and implementing responsive, high-performance front-end solutions. This role combines strong design skills with modern front-end development expertise to deliver seamless digital products.

Responsibilities
  • Design and develop responsive, user-friendly web interfaces using modern frameworks.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups for new features and products.
  • Implement clean, maintainable, and reusable code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript/TypeScript.
  • Collaborate with product managers and developers to define and enhance user experiences.
  • Ensure accessibility, cross-browser compatibility, and mobile-first design principles.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions.
  • Continuously improve UI/UX standards, staying updated with design and development trends.
Requirements
  • Proven experience as a Front-End Developer and/or UI/UX Designer.
  • Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript/TypeScript (React, Next.js, or similar frameworks).
  • Strong understanding of responsive and mobile-first design principles.
  • Experience with design tools such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
  • Knowledge of accessibility standards (WCAG) and best practices.
  • Familiarity with REST APIs, version control (Git), and modern development workflows.
  • Strong collaboration, communication, and problem-solving skills.
Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Design, HCI, or related field (or equivalent practical experience).
  • 3+ years of professional experience in front-end development and/or UI/UX design.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing both design and development projects.
  • Bonus:
    • Experience with motion design and animation (Framer Motion, Lottie).
    • Familiarity with CMS platforms (WordPress, Headless CMS).
    • Understanding of SEO and performance optimization techniques.

If you are interested in this position, please send your CV and the reason why you would like to join Chipglobe to: employment@chipglobe.com
