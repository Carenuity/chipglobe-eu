ChipGlobe Embedded Systems Services

Welcome to ChipGlobe's Embedded Systems Services page. At ChipGlobe, we combine deep industry knowledge with cutting-edge technologies to provide tailored embedded systems solutions for a wide range of applications. Our goal is to bring the highest standards of quality and innovation to every project, from initial concept to final deployment.

Our Expertise in Embedded Systems

ChipGlobe offers a comprehensive range of embedded systems services that meet the needs of various industries, including automotive, industrial automation, IoT, and more. We focus on delivering robust, scalable, and efficient embedded solutions tailored to meet each client's specific needs. Our team of experts works on everything from hardware design to software integration, ensuring a complete solution that is optimized for performance, power, and reliability.

  • Embedded Software Development: We develop custom software solutions for embedded systems, including low-level drivers, firmware, and middleware, with a focus on reliability, scalability, and efficiency
  • Hardware-Software Co-Design: Our team specializes in designing hardware and software components in tandem, ensuring optimal integration and functionality of embedded solutions.
  • RTOS & Bare-Metal Development: We provide real-time operating system (RTOS) development as well as bare-metal solutions, depending on the requirements for speed and control in your application.
  • Firmware Optimization: We focus on energy efficiency and real-time performance, optimizing firmware for different microcontrollers and processors to ensure the highest possible efficiency.
  • System Integration and Testing: We handle integration from hardware to software, and perform extensive testing to ensure that your embedded systems are reliable and perform as expected, including automated testing setups.

Tools and Technologies We Use

Our embedded systems team utilizes state-of-the-art tools and technologies to deliver the best possible solutions for our clients:

  • Programming Languages: Proficiency in C, C++, Python, and Assembly, tailored for specific platforms and system requirements.
  • RTOS Expertise: Deep experience in FreeRTOS, Zephyr, ThreadX, and other real-time operating systems to create scalable and reliable solutions.
  • Microcontrollers and Processors: Our team is skilled in working with various MCUs and MPUs, including those from STMicroelectronics, NXP, Microchip, and Texas Instruments.

Global Presence and Local Expertise

With design centers across the globe, ChipGlobe is well-positioned to provide support wherever our clients need us. Our locations include Munich (Germany), Dresden (Germany), Belgrade (Serbia), Thessaloniki (Greece), Sofia (Bulgaria), Singapore, and Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam). Each of our Offshore Development Centers (ODCs) works closely with clients to provide a local touch combined with our international expertise. This distributed structure allows us to offer customized solutions that cater to the specific needs of each region.
