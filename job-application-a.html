AI & Full-Stack Software Engineer (Rust, JavaScript, LangChain)
Job description

We are seeking a highly skilled AI & Full-Stack Software Engineer with expertise in Rust, JavaScript/TypeScript, and LangChain to join our innovative team. In this role, you will design, build, and optimize intelligent software systems that integrate AI-driven applications, scalable full-stack platforms, and blockchain/IoT solutions. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to deliver secure, high-performance, and user-focused products. This position is ideal for an engineer who thrives at the intersection of AI, full-stack web development, and cutting-edge systems engineering.

Responsibilities
  • Design, develop, and deploy AI-powered applications using LangChain, Genkit, and LLM APIs.
  • Build and maintain full-stack web solutions with modern frameworks such as Next.js, Nest.js, and Django.
  • Develop and optimize backend services, APIs, and microservices in Rust, Python, and TypeScript.
  • Implement secure authentication, authorization, and data protection across systems.
  • Design and deploy smart contracts and blockchain-based applications using Rust.
  • Integrate IoT devices and real-time data pipelines into intelligent software solutions.
  • Collaborate with product managers, designers, and fellow engineers to ship scalable, user-friendly products.
  • Conduct code reviews, testing, and performance optimization for production-grade systems.
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging AI frameworks, blockchain ecosystems, and software engineering practices.
Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience).
  • Proven experience in Rust, JavaScript/TypeScript, and Python for backend and full-stack development.
  • Hands-on expertise with LangChain, Genkit, or similar LLM orchestration frameworks.
  • Strong background in API development, system architecture, and microservices.
  • Experience with Next.js, Nest.js, or Django for building web applications.
  • Knowledge of blockchain development and smart contracts using Rust.
  • Familiarity with IoT systems integration and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
  • Solid understanding of software security, authentication, and performance optimization.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills, teamwork, and communication abilities.
Qualifications
  • Contributions to open-source AI or blockchain projects.
  • Experience with DevOps, CI/CD, and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Knowledge of GraphQL and real-time communication protocols (WebSockets, MQTT).
  • Background in image processing, data engineering, or ML model deployment.
What We Offer
  • Opportunity to work on cutting-edge AI and blockchain projects.
  • A collaborative and innovation-driven work culture.
  • Professional growth through continuous learning and emerging tech adoption.
  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Flexible work arrangements (remote/hybrid depending on location).
