Design Services

Design Services

Depending on the client needs, we are offering a wide range of services in order to conduct the design process in a given setup, while respecting the project milestones. Having worked with a variety of clients, from startups to large semiconductor companies, we are able to provide consulting and support in the planning stages of a project as well as to jump in and help the ongoing design process in the optimal way.

Languages, tools and more

Whether required design from scratch, maintenance of existing design or 3rd party IP integration, our designers are ready to support the client to excellence. While excelling in VHDL, Verilog and SystemVerilog hardware description languages we also provide support in mixed language designs with state-of-the-art EDA tools.

Languages

  • VHDL
  • Verilog
  • System Verilog
  • Perl, TCL, Makefile and Python scripting

Design categories

  • RTL design
  • Lint, CDC and RDC checks
  • Synthesis
  • Logic equivalence checks (LEC)
  • Functional safety (ISO 26262)

Tools

  • Cadence
  • Synopsys
  • Mentor
  • JIRA and other bug/issue tracking tools

Different levels and approaches

Our design experts are always ready to share their experience and help starting from the device concept phase through defining safety mechanisms to the final sign-off timing closure phase. The optimal IP architectures and micro architectures which save time during development and maintenance cycles are products of our experienced design engineers. Our designers always take care about trade off against area, power, effort and performance. Having experience in working on projects with various clients our design team offers support for design flow and methodology improvements.

Last, but not the least important, our designers provide structured and documented RTL designs with limitless possibilities of re-use and upgrades in the next product generations.

Our digital design engineers have exceptional problem-solving and analytical skills and always look at the big picture. Moreover, they deal with their daily work in a pragmatic and enthusiastic way and communicate clearly and openly.

  • Verification environment development/transition/upgrade
  • Test development, transition, and/or upgrade
  • 3rd party VIP usage and/or VIP development
  • Metric driven functional verification
  • Coverage closure
